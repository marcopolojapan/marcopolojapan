About US

Who We Are

MARCOPOLO JAPAN was established in 2015 and has basically been exporting used cars. We can always buy cars directly from Japanese auctions and offer them to our customers. Any car model can be offered to you. Not only automobiles but also all kinds of traded goods such as used bicycles and used clothes can be provided.

How We Do

When you want cars then please contact us and share with us your requirement.

When we receive your order then we will find the cars and inform you car details.

We will find the cars from auction and our business channels.
→　In this point it must be matched with market prices(Which type of cars with how much)

Payment must be advanced payment. When confirmed we will send you the proforma INVOICE. After received the INVOICE send the money within 3 working days (Within 3 working days money must be reach our bank account)

Above 3 points cleared then we will purchase the cars.

After purchasing the cars we will make shipping process immediately.
(※Shipping fees will be different with container shipping or unit shipping)

When cars shipped then we will send the Bill of Lading. After Bill of Lading arrived and also car arrived deal will be completed.
